A quote I read some time ago by Jordan Peterson

January 11, 2026

One day I was reading an article about Jordan Peterson. In this article there were many quotes made by the man himself, but one really stuck with me:

"Assume that the person you are listening to might know something you don't."

This reminds me to truly listen to others, with the idea that they might know something I don't yet. And this changed the way I started listening to people. Before, I was extremely bias and I frequently, unconsciously decided this person I am talking to is a blabberer. But by rewiring my brain I started listening differently. Soon I was able to extract more wisdom out of each interaction.

While I haven't mastered this and I a lot of times still listen half to someone, I encourage all of you to try this out.
