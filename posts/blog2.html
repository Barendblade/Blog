Have you ever had those sudden epiphanies. Those moments when the puzzle suddenly just become clear. Today when I sat outside and observed our garden with is verdant landscape, I thought of a few things

Not my house

Nature Nurture

What my eyes was observing was obviously not real nature. But still it remains plants and trees. With the smoothly cut grass and the birds in the trees, I did get somewhat of a taste.

And this is an idea I have recently grown more towards. I really do believe nature can nurture.

Anyhow nature definetely calms one down. Our busy lives are yelling for some off-time. Off-time is not watching a movie or doomscrolling, rather it is a moment for you body to really refresh. I do exhort each one of you to grasp this wisdom. Make time, at least once a week, to fully shutdown. This evolves into another topic I am passionate about; Boredom stirs creativity.

Someone more creditable also said the same

"There is no nature that exists devoid of nurture; there is no nurture that develops without nature," — Sir Francis Galton
